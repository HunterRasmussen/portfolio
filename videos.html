Artwork

My brother got me into movie making early on and since then it's been my favorite way to tell stories and express creativity. I spend most of my freetime in high school putting in too many hours on video projects. Many of my films won awards at Slickrock Film Festival, a central valley film competition and one of my films won best technical work at a state film festival.
